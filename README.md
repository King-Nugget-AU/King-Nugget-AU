# 👑 King Nugget

Based out of the Sydney Sector, I'm an independent game developer, audio engineer, and creator. I focus on bridging the gap between complex mechanics and engaging gameplay, while building heavy-hitting tracks rooted in Dubstep and Aussie rap.

Currently operating out of the Ableton Live Lite lab and mapping raw environmental Foley across the city. 

### 🕹️ Active Developments
* **[Axiom Syndicate: Infinite Hegemony](https://King-Nugget-AU.github.io/)** — My latest corporate idle simulation.
* **Chronicle: Adrenaline Engine** — Tactical text-based RPG interface (In Dev).
* **Nugget Bot** — Personalized AI executive assistant (In Dev).

### 🎧 Audio Archives
